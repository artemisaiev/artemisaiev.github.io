About This Channel

This channel is a modern remake of the classic 1980 graphic adventure game “Mystery House”, originally created by Ken and Roberta Williams and published by Sierra On-Line.

The original “Mystery House” game was released into the public domain by Sierra On-Line in 1987 as part of Sierra’s 7th anniversary celebration.

This remake was created as a tribute to the original game and to preserve and celebrate an important piece of computer gaming history.
